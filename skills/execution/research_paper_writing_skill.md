# Research Paper Writing

Follow standard IMRaD structure:
1. **Abstract:** Objective, Method, Result, Conclusion (strict 250 words max).
2. **Introduction:** State the gap in literature. No "Since the dawn of time" or "In today's fast-paced world" openings.
3. **Methods:** Active voice preferred for clarity ("We measured" not "It was measured").
4. **Results:** Facts only. Save interpretation for Discussion.
5. **Anti-Slop:** Zero AI jargon. Be terse. Be factual. No sycophancy.
